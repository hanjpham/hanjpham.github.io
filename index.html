Welcome

Hello! I'm a PhD student in Public Policy at the Brooks School of Public Policy, Cornell University. My research focuses on applied microeconomics, industrial organization, and public policies in health care markets.

I'm passionate about leveraging economic principles to understand and improve health care systems and policies. My work aims to contribute to more efficient and equitable health care delivery.

Research

Current Projects

  • Health Care Provider Behavior: Analyzing the impact of financial incentives on physician decision-making and patient outcomes in the U.S. health care system.
  • Insurance Market Dynamics: Investigating the effects of policy changes on insurance market competition and consumer welfare in the Affordable Care Act marketplaces.

Research Interests

  • Applied Microeconomics
  • Industrial Organization in Health Care Markets
  • Public Health Policy
  • Health Care Economics
  • Econometric Methods in Policy Evaluation
