Welcome

Hello! I'm a PhD student in Public Policy at the Brooks School of Public Policy, Cornell University. My research focuses on applied microeconomics, industrial organization, and public policies in health care markets.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I completed my bachelor's degree in Mathematical Economics at Temple University in Philadelphia. This background has provided me with a strong foundation in quantitative analysis and economic theory, which I now apply to my research in public policy and health care economics.

I'm passionate about leveraging economic principles to understand and improve health care systems and policies. My work aims to contribute to more efficient and equitable health care delivery.
