Welcome

I'm a PhD student in Public Policy at the Brooks School of Public Policy, Cornell University, with an expected graduation in Spring 2026. My research focuses on health economics, industrial organization, and policy analysis, with particular emphasis on healthcare markets and policy implementation.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I completed my bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics with honors at Temple University.
